About Us

Welcome to Bon Jour Pinoy Restaurant a virtual restaurant website page created by Bon Jury Pecaoco, an experienced frontend developer. This website was developed as a showcase and for future inspiration. Please note that all content here is fictional and is not intended for real-world application.

My Journey

My journey started with a simple mission: to build a first working website that demonstrates how innovative solutions can empower individuals and businesses to grow.

This project is dedicated to inspiring those with limited resources but who are passionate about entering the world of programming. Using only a modest setup, I want to show that with commitment and determination, you can achieve anything you set your mind to.

As of July 2025 I decide to update this website as a fully working website that can be use if needed by small to medium size business. I am proud to say that this website is fully functional and can be used for real-world applications.

Looking Ahead

Whether you’re here to visit, leave feedback, or simply explore, I’m always open to new ideas that could further enhance this website’s functionality in the tech world.

As I continue my journey as a developer, I am eager to grow my skills, particularly in the future.

Achievements

In a 1 month (September to October) of solo development, I built a full-stack restaurant website entirely on an Android phone. This involved significant code changes with only 1 author (3,691 additions, 1,179 deletions across 58 files) and frequent commits (147 to main) while learning frontend and backend technologies, including Firebase integration. This accomplishment highlights my rapid learning, problem-solving, and dedication to delivering a functional website despite limited resources.
